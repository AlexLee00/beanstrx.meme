SUNBEANS STORY

Breaking News!
A massive meteor is hurtling toward Earth!
If this colossal rock makes impact, it could mean the end of all life on the planet!

Dr. Sun has called a press conference to share an urgent message about the future of humanity. Many are tuned in, and—ah, Dr. Sun is speaking now. Let’s listen in. “Our research team has studied the meteor heading towards Earth, and we have concluded that there is a 99.2% chance of collision. Should this occur, all living creatures on the planet will not survive. In light of this, we must strive to leave behind a testament to our existence. Ladies and gentlemen, we aim to preserve the great artistic works of humanity as evidence of our presence. We have named this project TRON. It is designed to ensure that all artworks retain their value and can testify to human history. I implore you to help us safeguard as many works of art as possible.”

Following Dr. Sun's speech, countless people—and even some non-human entities—came forward, bringing the art they had cherished most. Each one arrived at the storage facility for Project TRON, eager to preserve their chosen pieces. So many artworks were collected that the warehouse quickly became full. Dr. Sun, deeply grateful for their participation, personally oversaw the listing of each piece they brought. The meteor's impact was now imminent. Despite the ticking clock, efforts were made to secure as many works as possible. One day, someone—or something—would look upon these artifacts and know that humanity once existed. These artworks would not lose their value. They would endure, a testament to the greatness of humankind.

In the end, the meteor delivered a deep and fateful kiss to Earth. As a result, no living creature could be found anywhere on the planet. The AI [TRON], stationed in Dr. Sun's lab, continued scanning for life signals, but none remained. Following its programming, [TRON] expanded its search beyond Earth, scanning the vast reaches of space for any sign of life. Before long, [TRON] detected a life signal aboard a space station not far from Earth. It hacked into their systems and sent a distress message. The lifeforms, known as SUNBEANS, saw the message and made a decision—to help. They immediately launched a spacecraft toward Earth.
